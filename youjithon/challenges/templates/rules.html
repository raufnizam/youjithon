Discord Rules

1. No Racism/Hate Speech

• Racism, sexism, etc. will not be tolerated in this server.

• Also keep custom statuses clean. If the status breaks TOS we will kick you.

2. Do not send Malicious Content

• Sending/Linking any harmful material such as viruses, IP grabbers, etc. will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

• Any discussions regarding development of malicious software will also result in immediate punishment with no warning.

• We ask that you do not upload python files when sharing your code. Stick to web-services such as Hastebin, Mystbin or python-discords: paste.pythondiscord.com.

3. No NSFW in any of the chats

• Please keep anything NSFW out of this server.

4. No spreading/leaking Personal Information

• Please do not threaten or attempt to dox any members of this server.

• It is against Discord TOS and will result in a permanent ban from this server.

5. No Advertising

• This goes for Twitch, Discord, YouTube, etc.

• If your main purpose is to come to this server and Advertise you will be banned.

6. No Punishment Evasion

• Do not attempt to evade any punishment you receive.

• If you are caught doing it, it will result in a kick/ban.

7. Refrain from any conversations discussing politics, etc.

• This is a coding discord, not a politics discord.

• If you & another member want to speak about topics related to politics, you can talk about it in #random-chat or DM’s.

8. Please speak English in this server.

9. Please respect Discord’s Terms of Service

• Anything that breaks these terms will not be tolerated and will result in a ban.

• Discord Terms of Service

• Discord Guidelines

10. Also, please do not ping Tim.

These are the server rules and they will be enforced.
